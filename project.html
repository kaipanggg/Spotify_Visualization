Emotions and Music Throughout the Years

Presented by Kai Pang and Catherine Tao





For our project, we chose to focus on different attributes for musical tracks which we analyze to determine an individual's feelings and emotions. The different attributes we chose to analyze include valence, tempo, danceability, loudness, and energy.

Valence: Describes the positiveness within a song analyzed on a scale between 0 to 1. High valence is considered positive (energetic, cheerful) while low valence is considered negative (sad, depressing)

Tempo: Describes the speed of the track in terms of beats per minute, measured on a scale between 0 to 1

Danceability: Descibres how danceable a track is based on features such as beat, rhythm, and tempo. Measured on a scale between 0 to 1.

Loudness: Describes how loud a track is presented in decibals. Measured on a scale between 0 to 1.

Energy: Describes the intensity and activity of a track based on features such as tempo and loudness. Measured on a scale between 0 to 1.
