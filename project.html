Emotions in Time

Throughout the past century, Spotify data shows that people's musical preference based on valence levels have changed. Between 1921-1953 we see that the graph is volatile while the years proceeding appear more stable.

A Fast Change in Music

Proportions of fast and slow songs are roughly equal in the year with lowest median valence (1946), whereas fast songs dominate in higher valence years (1979). Though valence of music has been decreasing, people nowadays generally prefer music with faster tempo.



Changes in Popular Songs

Despite the 20 year difference, the top popular songs of the years displayed focus on different attributes of music. In 1980, Call Me focused on energy, valence, and tempo. In 2000, Breathe focused on tempo, danceability, and energy. In 2019, Old Town Road focused on danceability and loudness.
